
The gentle breeze whispers, heralding the spring.
A quiet dawn song drifts across the silent earth.
Flowers dance and sing their fragrant melodies.
The spring sunset paints in gold,
and its song carries into the evening.

The spring wind softly brushes through the walker's hair,
and birds soar high, singing in mysterious tones.
A still lake holding dew whispers lullabies to young trees.
And spring ends with the sound of bells, as sunlight gently recedes.
